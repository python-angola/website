About

Building a stronger Python community for Angola.

Python Angola connects students, developers, educators, researchers, and professionals with workshops, mentorship, open-source projects, and events designed to grow technical capacity throughout Angola.

Focus Areas

What we are building.

👥

Community-first events

Meetups, tech circles, study groups, and community projects that bring people together.

🎓

Learning and mentorship

Beginner-friendly courses, study paths, and mentorship for students and aspiring developers.

💡

AI and data science

Practical AI, automation, and data science training that can accelerate Angola’s digital future.

🌍

Open source impact

Collaborative projects, code sharing, and community-led initiatives that make a visible impact.

Roadmap

Roadmap for the first year.

Phase 1

Launch the site and share the vision.

Phase 2

Host learning experiences.

Phase 3

Build community and projects.

Support collaboration, open-source work, and practical learning across Angola.
